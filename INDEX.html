The Hotel KING

A BOUTIQUE HOTEL & GATHERING SPACE IN PARISH, THE HOTEL KING

The Island House stands out among The Hotel King resorts, having been designed with both visitors and the local community in mind, and as a symbiotic extension of the surrounding environment. The property features 30 rooms and suites, six rental apartments, two restaurants, a cafe, wine market, art house cinema, lap pool, movement studio, squash courts, TERN art gallery and a Bamford Spa.
